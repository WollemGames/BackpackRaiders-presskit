Pitch

Backpack Raiders is a hybrid survival roguelite + tactical inventory builder where how you pack your gear matters as much as what you bring.

Build your loadout. Outplay the swarm. Thrive in the city.

Pack Your Power –
Your backpack is your build. Every weapon, trinket, attachment, and oddity fits into a grid-based inventory where position, adjacency, and orientation can make or break a run.

Battle The Bugs –
Fight the swarm in short 30–60 second rounds. Collect rewards to buy more items and supplies before diving back into the city. See how long you can last and climb the leaderboards by comparing the top builds to your own.
Pack smarter. Live Longer.

Build Your Outpost –
Between raids, expand your outpost with workshops, armories, trading posts, and other specialty structures. Each building grants powerful bonuses, unlocks new options, and improves your starting loadouts. Building placement matters; organize your outpost to unlock powerful synergies.

Customize Your Difficulty –
Choose your challenge with modular difficulty modifiers, from tougher enemies to higher shop prices. Push the difficulty to earn better rewards, special items, and more resources.

Merc For Hire –
Pick a mercenary that fits your playstyle, from a gunslinging bandit to a thug frog, each with their own strengths and starting options.
